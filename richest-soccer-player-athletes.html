Argentinian footballer Javier Mascherano has an estimated net worth of $40 million. He currently plays as the defensive midfielder for the Argentina national team and the center back for Hebei China Fortune.

In 2003–04, Javier Mascherano earned his first senior honors after he won the Primera División Argentina. He was a part of the Argentina team which participated in the Athens Games and won the Olympic gold medal. He was also a runner-up at the Copa América 2004.

Javier Alejandro Mascherano was born on June 8, 1984, in San Lorenzo, Argentina. Since 2008, Mascherano has been married to Fernanda Mascherano with whom he has two children named Alma and Lola. In Argentina, Javier Mascherano is known for his two nicknames, El Jefecito and Masche.
