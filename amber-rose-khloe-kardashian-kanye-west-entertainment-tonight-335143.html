Amber Rose Talks Khloe Kardashian And Kanye West On 'ET'

Amber Rose wants the whole world to know she does not hate Khloe Kardashian. In a recent interview with Entertainment Tonight, she revealed she has no bad blood against the Kardashian clan. She also went on to address Kanye West’s infamous “30 showers” statement on The Breakfast Club.

The feud between Khloe and Muva started back in February when Amber went on The Breakfast Club and addressed how she felt about Kylie Jenner and Tyga’s relationship.

“That’s ridiculous,” Rose said of the alleged relationship. “[Tyga] should be ashamed of himself. For sure. He has a beautiful woman and a baby and left that for a 16-year-old who just turned 17.”

SEE ALSO: Amber Rose Is Organizing A ‘SlutWalk’ For Women’s Equality

Amber admitted, however, that this scuffle could have been prevented had she not stated her opinion on Khloe’s younger sister’s relationship.

“I could have totally minded my own business when it came to her sister,” Rose explained. “But, me being a dancer at such a young age, dealing with older men gave me the humility and knowledge to speak on such a subject. (Yet) I can understand why it came across the wrong way to her. I get it. But, that’s a phone call she could have had with me.”

Then the 31 year-old model later went on to profess how she felt about Kanye’s statement.

“We were happy when we were together!” Rose said. “And now all of the sudden, I’m getting slut-shamed because we’re not together anymore and it’s not fair.”
