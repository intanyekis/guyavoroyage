A Man From Central Pennsylvania Was Found Guilty Of Molesting Several Young Girls: The York County district attorney’s office announced on Friday that a jury had found 43-year-old Brody Barrett Kline guilty of 25 counts of child sex abuse.

The DA’s office claims Kline is guilty of multiple crimes, including rape of a child, involuntary deviant sexual intercourse with a minor younger than 13, aggravated indecent assault, incest, and others.

According to court documents obtained by the York Dispatch, DNA evidence linked Kline to the sexual abuse of multiple females between the ages of 4 and 14, leading to the filing of formal accusations.

According to the York Dispatch and court papers, the abuse went on for more than a year until two of the girls told their mother in 2019. The mother confronted Kline and thought it would end the abuse at the moment, but authorities claim it continued until October 2020, when the mother found semen in one of the girls’ underwear.

Kline’s DNA was linked to the molestation of two more minors in July 2021, the York Dispatch said

The York County District Attorney’s Office said in a statement on Friday that “this was a great effort by investigators and prosecutors to find some sense of justice for these young children and their families.”

Kline’s sentencing is set for February 14, 2023, but not until a sexual offender assessment is conducted on him.
