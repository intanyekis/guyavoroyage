Monica Potter Disses Iggy Azalea At People’s Choice Awards — Calls Her Igloo

Take a look at the video below, which shows Monica, 43, and her Parenthood co-star Dax Shepard, 40, presenting the award for favorite hip hop artist.

Monica flubs several of the nominees’ names, calling one Dr. Drew (it’s not even clear who she means, considering the list did not include Dr. Dre) and then referring to Iggy as “Igloo Australia.” Yikes! While it is possible that the mispronunciation was simply a joke, it’s hard to give Monica a pass considering the ENTIRE WORLD has been talking about Azealia’s war on “Igloo Australia.”

Iggy seemed to return Monica’s diss when she took the stage to accept the award for favorite hip hop artist and totally flirted with Dax, while giving his co-star the cold shoulder.

Azealia Banks Attacks Iggy Azalea On Twitter Over Eric Garner Case

Azealia Banks, 23, slammed Iggy in December for exploiting black culture yet staying “silent” on important issues like the Eric Garner case.

After a New York grand jury decided not to indict Daniel Pantaleo in Eric’s death on Dec. 3, Azealia took to Twitter to call out Iggy, saying:

its funny to see people Like Igloo Australia silent when these things happen… Black Culture is cool, but black issues sure aren’t huh?

— CATTY NOIR (@AZEALIABANKS) December 4, 2014

And that was just the beginning. She tweeted several more messages, including this one:

And the media needs to STOP trying to move igloo in on what Nicki has established. — CATTY NOIR (@AZEALIABANKS) December 4, 2014
