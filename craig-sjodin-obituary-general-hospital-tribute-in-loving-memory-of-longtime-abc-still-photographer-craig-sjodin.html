Craig Sjodin Obituary: General Hospital tribute in loving memory of longtime ABC still photographer

Craig Sjodin Obituary – Cause of Death: General Hospital tribute in loving memory of longtime ABC still photographer Craig Sjodin.

Following the General Hospital episode on Wednesday, September 27, a tribute post was made in loving memory of Craig Sjodin who played a key role to General Hospital as an ABC still photographer for the last 39 years.

Sjodin passed away on September 15 from wounds sustained in a bicycling accident.

His obituary stated that he was looking forward to taking his family to Australia and other locations that he had seen over his career. He had recently resigned from the position that benefited greatly from his keen eye. He is survived by numerous people, including his son, sisters, and wife of 30 years.

General Hospital is an American daytime television soap opera. It is listed in Guinness World Records as the longest-running American soap opera in production, and the second in American history after Guiding Light. Concurrently, it is the world’s third longest-running scripted drama series in production after British serials The Archers and Coronation Street, as well as the world’s second-longest-running televised soap opera still in production.
