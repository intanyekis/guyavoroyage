Chaz Lamar Shepherd Net Worth

Chaz Lamar Shepherd Net Worth: $1 Million

American actor and singer-songwriter Chaz Lamar Shepherd has a net worth of $1 million. He was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania in October 1977. Shepherd starred in a national tour of the musical Dreamgirls from 2009 to 2010 as Curtis Taylor, Jr. He also starred in the television series Me and the Boys from 1994 to 1995 as Artis Tower, and from 1996 to 2001 he starred as Josh Hamilton in the TV series 7th Heaven.

Shepherd has had recurring roles in various TV series including Minor Adjustments, Sister, Sister, In the House, The Parkers, and The Game. He has also starred in movies like The Nutty Professor, Set it Off, and Woman Thou Art Loosed. As a singer, Shepherd released the album Love & Truth in 2010.
