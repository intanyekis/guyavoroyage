Retired Professional American football player Andre Johnson has an estimated net worth of $20 million. He played in the National Football League for the Houston Texans, Indianapolis Colts, and Tennessee Titans as the teams’ wide receiver. In 2012, he received an annual salary amounting to $700 thousand.

Andre Lamont Johnson was born on July 11, 1981, in Miami, Florida. He attended the University of Miami. Eventually, he was selected by the Texans as a first round (third overall) pick of the 2003 NFL Draft. Since then, he has been playing in the National Football League for the Texans.

In 2003, he agreed to a six-year $39 million contract with the Houston Texans with guaranteed compensation of more than $13.501 million. In August 2010, he signed a two-year extension contract until 2016 which made him as the highest paid wide receiver in the history of the National Football League.

Throughout his entire ten-year NFL career, Johnson has been selected to Pro Bowl six times (2004, 2006, 2008-2010, 2012). He was also a four-time team MVP of the Texans (2006, 2008-2010). Furthermore, Johnson was named as the NFL Alumni Wide Receiver of the Year three times.
