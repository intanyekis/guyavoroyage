Twitter Users Are Loving Tucker Carlson Being Put On Blast As Montana Man Calls Him The 'Worst Human

Out of all the states to have someone call out Fox News' Tucker Carlson out, the man from Montana named Dan Bailey is perhaps one of the bravest people to dub Carlson as the worst person alive. Montana is more conservative than liberal, but that doesn't mean there aren't any left leaning citizens that reside in the mountainous state. Bailey would later post his confrontation with the political commentator on his Instagram page.

Ever since this viral video came out, many Twitter users enjoyed the Fox News representative being roasted in a state where he least expected it.

Bailey wrote on Instagram, "It’s not everyday you get to tell someone they are the worst person in the world and really mean it! What an a**hole! This man has killed more people with vaccine misinformation, he has supported extreme racism, he is a fascist and does more to rip this country apart than anyone that calls themselves an American."

When confronted by Bailey, Carlson whispered to the store owner to keep it down, saying, "Settle down, son." Bailey was not having it, and told him not to call him that as the video ends seconds later.

Other than Carlson, there have been other controversial commentators from Fox News, such as Megyn Kelly and Tomi Lahren who have also been criticized for their less than ideal takes on political and social issues.

There have been debates on Twitter whether it was necessary to do something like this, even if it seems warranted for bringing criticism to the Tucker Carlson Tonight host. The majority of Twitter, however, is on board with seeing Carlson get ratted out.

Users such as @hornfels called the whole incident "based," praising Bailey for standing his own ground and not showing cowardice whatsoever. Another user, @guido_father, believes that confronting celebrities like him should be the norm due to the misinformation Carlson brought regarding the pandemic and his alleged racism as mentioned by Bailey.

Others like @shlliep64 have been calling Bailey a national treasure for his bravery and giving them entertainment as they replayed the clip many times. Some even noted that Carlson looked afraid and knew that he was being called out, despite his attempts to mediate the situation.

This won't be the first time a conservative political commentator will get called out and caught on video, and it definitely won't be the last.
