Radamel Falcao Net Worth

Radamel Falcao is a Colombian professional soccer player who currently plays for AS Monaco in the French Ligue 1 and represents the Colombia national football team. He has a net worth of $70 million and is considered one of the top strikers in the world.

At the age of 13, Falcao made his professional debut as an attacker for Lanceros Boyaca, becoming the youngest debutant at that level of Colombian soccer. He then signed with River Plate, where he played from 2005 to 2009 and scored 45 goals. He signed with Porto in 2009, scoring 34 and 38 goals over two seasons, respectively. His hot streak continued with Atletico Madrid from 2011 to 2013, where he scored 36 and 34 goals in his two seasons.

In 2012, Falcao signed with Monaco, where he has been nicknamed El Tigre, which translates to The Tiger, as well as King of the Europa League. He helped lead F.C. Porto to a Europe League title and was recognized with the Portuguese Golden Ball award in 2011. Falcao won his first EUFA Super Cup in 2012 and scored a hat trick, becoming the first player in history to do so. Additionally, he had the remarkable achievement of scoring five goals in a single game in 2012.

Falcao has been playing on the Colombian national team since 2007 and finished as Colombia’s top goal scorer with nine goals during the 2014 FIFA World Cup qualification rounds.
