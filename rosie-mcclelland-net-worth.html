Rosie McClelland Net Worth, Career, YouTube, Facts

Rosie McClelland is an English child actress, television personality, and author from Essex, England. McClelland is one of the members of the famous child duo, Sophia Grace & Rosie. The duo rose to fame after being featured in The Ellen DeGeneres Show regularly as their cover video of the Nicki Minaj‘s song Super Bass went viral on Youtube. The video has 54 million views as of December 2019.

Rosie McClelland is the cousin of Sophia Grace Brownlee, the other member of the Duo, who is considered much bolder and outgoing. Rosie is three years younger than her counterpart.

Who is Rosie McClelland?

Rosie McClelland was born on 7th September 2006 in Essex, UK. McClelland has a younger sibling, Romeo McClelland born in 2014, who occasionally appears on her Instagram.

Rosie witnessed her parents’ wedding in 2015 and served as a bridesmaid for the ceremony.

Rosie lives in Los Angeles, California, the United States of America, along with her mother, Danielle Brownlee, and her younger brother, while her father Greg McClelland stays in the UK. He splits his time between Los Angeles and the UK.

Net Worth of Rosie McClelland

The child prodigy was just five years old when she formed the Duo Sophia Grace & Rosie with her cousin Sophia. The UK based Duo posted the cover video of Super Bass by Nicki Minaj on 19th September 2011, which went viral instantly and paved the path for the Duo to the Hollywood limelight.

Rosie was invited with her cousin to The Ellen DeGeneres Show on 12 October 2011 and later on became recurring cast members on the show. The girls hosted their segment named Tea Time with Sophia Grace & Rosie, where they interviewed celebrities like Miley Cyrus, Taylor Swift, Rihanna, Katy Perry, Hugh Grant, Julie Bowen, Harry Connick Jr., LL Cool J, Justin Bieber, and Reese Witherspoon. The segment won the duo Choice Webstar at the 2012 Teen Choice Award.

McClelland also hosted interviews with her cousin as a member of Tea Time with Sophia Grace & Rosie in major award shows like American Music Awards, Billboard Music Award, and Grammy Award Twice.

Rosie also featured along with Sophia in Nickelodeon hit Sam & Cat in two episodes, where the pair worked with Ariana Grande and Jennette McCurdy.

The little performer also went on to star in her very own film with Sophia entitled Sophia Grace and Rosie’s Royal Adventure under Warner Bros Production in Spring 2014. The studio inked a five-figure deal with Rosie and her cousin Sophia.

The British Duo was also due to be featured in their very own show on NBC by Warner Bros Television and Ellen DeGeneres Productions on 2013. The show was to be written and developed by legendary Murphy Brown creator Diane English.

On January 2014, Rosie launched her very own merchandising campaign as part of the Sophia Grace and Rosie team. A collaboration with leading toy manufacturers Just Play, the line featured the finest in princess wear, including tiaras, tutus, dresses, and fashion dolls. 

In between it all, she released her very first book, Tea Time With Sophia Grace and Rosie. It hit #2 on the New York Times Bestseller’s list and #6 on Amazon.com’s Top Books list. Two more Sophia Grace and Rosie books are in the works for Scholastic.

After the split of the Duo in 2014, Rosi started focusing on her singing and youtube channel named after her with 766 thousand subscribers and her Instagram rosiergm with 615 thousand followers. She released her first song titled Handstand on her channel on May 26, 2018, which has a view of 8,047,132 and her second song LaLa with the view of 976,262 as of December 2019.

Rosie McClelland has estimated net worth of $250,000 after summing up the earnings of her career in the Hollywood showbiz for nearly five years along with her Youtube channel, which earned her $180,000 until now. Rosie also endorses several clothing line products along with her own merchandise on Instagram with 615,000 followers, helping her collect more greens from stacking up her net worth.
