Biography

Martin Compston was born in Greenock, Scotland on May 8, 1984. Former professional soccer player turned actor best known for his role as Detective Sergeant Steve Arnott in the crime drama Line of Duty. He previously played Ewan Brodie in Monarch of the Glen. In Line of Duty, he starred opposite Vicky McClure and Adrian Dunbar. Before making his acting debut in 2002’s Sweet Sixteen, he played youth soccer for Greenock Morton and would briefly play for their professional team.
