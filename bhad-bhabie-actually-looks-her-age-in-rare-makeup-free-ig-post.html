Bhad Bhabie Actually Looks Her Age In Rare Makeup Free IG Post

Since rocketing to fame at 13 on that unforgettable 'Dr. Phil' episode, Danielle Bregoli aka Bhad Bhabie has launched a music career, befriended A-Listers, and bought a Porsche when she wasn't old enough to drive.

Now it's a few years later and this diva still isn't even old enough to vote. She turned 17 in March 2020, making her the exact same age as JoJo Siwa.

Unlike kid-friendly JoJo, Bhad Bhabie usually looks WAY OLDER than your average high schooler - but a video promoting her new single just showed her in a different light. For once she's looking like a fresh faced teenager and fans are here for it.

Danielle Usually Goes Full Glam

This girl is not afraid of cosmetics. From thick lashes to long acrylics and that trademark red ponytail weave, Bhad Bhabie is rarely seen without a good amount of glam on.

In her new music video, 'Do It Like Me', Bhad Bhabie's being her typical dolled-up self. She dropped the video yesterday after weeks of hyping it on her socials. It features all the lip liner and fake lashes that fans have come to expect from this young diva.

Her Next Post Made Her Look like a 'Tiny Teen' in Comparison

Blasting 'Do It Like Me' in a room full of music awards and designer merch, Danielle's latest video shows her popping out of a Dior shopping bag with notably less elaborate hair and makeup. Fans are saying she looks adorable, tiny, and hella young.

"Dior selling children now," joked @seabass_sized on Bhad Bhabie's post.

Fans reacting with "You're adorable" and "tiny teen" earned likes that shot them to the top of the comments section, too.

Lots of fans responded with positive comments encouraging Bhad Bhabie to try going makeup-free more in the future, calling her "mad cute" with her natural look. One liked it enough to ask "you 18 yet?" They don't call her 'Babyface Savage' for nothing, but in this case her baby face is proof that no dude, this kid hiding in a shopping bag is not a legal adult yet.

The cutest response? Someone said Danielle should change her name to "wholesome bhabie." With a restraining order against her we don't see that happening anytime soon, but aw.
