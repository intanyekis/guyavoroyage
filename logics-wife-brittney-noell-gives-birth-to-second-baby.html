Logic and wife Brittney Noell welcome their second baby: We couldnt be happier

Logic has another little one.

The rapper and his wife, Brittney Noell, have welcomed their second child.

“our beautiful Leo was born and we couldn’t be happier 🤍,” Noell captioned the newborn’s Instagram debut on Monday.

“we are all (especially Bobby) so in love,” she continued, referencing their eldest child.

The couple revealed in January Instagram posts that baby No. 2 was on the way. “Happy New Baby,” Logic, 33, captioned a video from an ultrasound appointment.

.

The Grammy nominee previously surprised fans with his son Bobby’s arrival in 2020.

“I wanted to share with you a glimpse into the life I am now diving into head first,” he wrote alongside family photos at the time. “I would like you to meet LB as we call him, Little Bobby.”

Logic, whose real name is Sir Robert Bryson Hall II, continued, “[He] makes me the happiest man in the world, and it’s a happiness nothing else has ever been able to fulfill.”

The reveal came days after the “1-800-273-8255” performer announced his retirement, saying his July 2020 “No Pressure” album would be his last.

Bobby’s subsequent Instagram debut also marked the first time Logic posted about Noell. He and the 26-year-old secretly tied the knot in September 2019.

The songwriter was previously married to singer Jessica Andrea from 2015 to 2018.

The former couple “came to the loving conclusion that [they were] better as friends,” Logic wrote of their divorce.

“It’s very simple: it just didn’t work out. There is no anger involved,” he explained. “No fighting, no cheating, no nothing. We love each other and will continue to support each other for the rest of our lives.”

Andrea, 30, showed their amicability at the time with a supportive comment, writing, “I love you Bobby … and love to all the fans!”
