Spencer Haywood net worth, age, height, wiki, wife, biography

The net worth, age, height, and wiki of Spencer Haywood are discussed in this article. Spencer Haywood, a former professional basketball player and Olympic gold medalist from the United States, achieved several accolades during his career, including being a member of the All-NBA First Team in 1972 and 1973, and the All-NBA Second Team in 1974 and 1975.

Spencer Haywood Biography & Profile Summary

Spencer Haywood Age & Wiki

Spencer Haywood was born on 22 April 1949 in Silver Metropolis, Mississippi, United States. He is approximately 74 years old. He holds American nationality and is known for his career as a former basketball player and Olympic gold medalist.

Known for his exceptional talent, Haywood began his career at a young age and demonstrated an early interest in sports, excelling in basketball during his time at Pershing High School, and later, Trinidad State Junior College. He achieved numerous accolades and was inducted into the Naismith Memorial Basketball Hall of Fame in 2015.

Spencer Haywood Height

Spencer Haywood stands at 6 feet 8 inches tall.

Spencer Haywood Career

Haywood’s basketball career spanned various teams and competitions, including the Denver Rockets of ABA, Seattle SuperSonics, New York Knicks, New Orleans Jazz, Los Angeles Lakers, Rayer Venezia, and NBA’s Washington Bullets. Notably, he was appointed #30 overall by the Buffalo Braves in the 1971 NBA draft, won an NBA championship in 1980, and was honored as the most valuable player in the American Basketball Association in 1970, among other achievements.

Moreover, Haywood’s impact on the game extended beyond his playing career, as he was integral in paving the way for underclassmen college basketball players to leave college early and play professionally—an initiative known as the “Spencer Haywood rule.”

Spencer Haywood Personal Life

Haywood was married to fashion model Iman from 1977 to 1987 and has a daughter named Zulekha Haywood. He later remarried Linda Haywood in 1990, with whom he has three children: Shaakira Haywood, Isis Haywood, and Courtney Nikkiah Haywood.

Spencer Haywood Assets

Despite numerous achievements, Haywood’s career faced challenges. Notably, he was offered the choice between $100,000 in cash or 10% of Nike’s entire company in its early years. Choosing the former, Haywood nonetheless became the first NBA player to secure a shoe deal with Nike, a decision that had groundbreaking implications for the industry.

Reflecting on this decision, Haywood could have potentially amassed immense wealth if he had chosen the 10% offer, which would be valued at $8.6 billion today. Despite this, his legacy as a Hall of Fame inductee remains enduring.

Spencer Haywood Net Worth
