Prince William, Harry, and Pippa Attend Wedding Without Kate Middleton

Pippa and the boys! Prince William, Prince Harry and Pippa Middleton were all spotted attending the wedding of James Meade and Lady Laura Marsham in Norfolk, England on Saturday, Sept. 14. The nuptials took place at The Parish Church of St. Nicholas Gayton, but was celebrated without one noticeably absent person — Kate Middleton! The Duchess of Cambridge originally planned on attending the ceremony, but decided against it earlier this week. 

“Kate’s still breast feeding so to be out for the whole day and the night before, was a bit too much at the moment,” a Middleton source tells Us Weekly. “She would have loved to go but it just wasn’t possible.”
