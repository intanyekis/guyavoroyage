Are Lydia and Milton still together?

With its novel idea of fast dating in ‘Pods,’ where relationships are made without ever seeing each other, Love Is Blind Season 5 grabbed the world by storm.

Lydia Arlene Velez Gonzalez and James Milton Johnson IV, commonly known as Lydia and Milton, were one of the five couples to come out of this unique dating experiment, and their emotional journey won viewers over. 

In this article, we examine Lydia and Milton’s enthralling love affair, looking at their chemistry, the difficulties they encountered, and, most significantly, the question on everyone’s lips: Are Lydia and Milton still together?

Who are Lydia and Milton? 

There were five couples on Love Is Blind Season 5, which debuted on Netflix in September 2023. Lydia Arlene Velez Gonzalez and James Milton Johnson IV were one of them. 

A speed-dating show called Love Is Blind follows about 30 men and women over a few weeks as they converse with one another but cannot see one another in “Pods,” where they speed-date.

There are 10 days for the couples to decide whether or not to get engaged without having met.

All about Love Is Blind Season 5 

Both participants and fans experience a wide range of emotions when watching Love Is Blind.

For candidates, the evident stress of going through a romantic relationship while filming for a popular television program with all its pressures causes obvious distress. 

Additionally, there is the difficulty of remaining silent after recording, keeping that a secret until the show is published, at which point viewers will learn the outcome of your relationship over a few weeks. 

Although there is somewhat less emotional tension for spectators, there is still excitement and emotional distress as they watch different individuals match up with persons they believe to be suited for them and watch to see if those relationships work out or dramatically fail.

Bond of Lydia and Milton

In the first in-depth talk we witness the potential matches on season five of the popular dating program, “Love Is Blind” stars Milton Johnson and Lydia Velez Gonzalez connect over rocks.

Even though she is a geologist and he is an engineer, their shared passion for science almost doesn’t enable them to bridge their age gap.

Lydia, 30, is a highly expressive emotional person who seeks a partner who respects her for who she is.

Milton, 24, on the other hand, ultimately reveals to Lydia that he finds it difficult to be honest and vulnerable with others but claims that she has assisted him in doing so.

Milton and Lydia had exciting sexual chemistry while on vacation in Mexico. However, when they get back to Houston, Texas, and have to encounter Uche during the cast reunion, reality bursts their love bubble.

Are Lydia and Milton still together?

No, Lydia and Milton are not together. There have not been any confirmations, but Lydia and Milton were not following one another on Instagram at the time of the fifth season premiere of Love Is Blind, it’s plausible that something occurred to them after their wedding. 

According to official records from the Harris County Clerk’s Office in Texas, where Houston is situated and Love Is Blind Season 5 was filmed, the only pair from Season 5 of Love Is Blind to wed was Lydia and Milton.

Lydia and Milton received their marriage license on May 13, 2022, were wed on May 31, 2022, and had their license processed and returned on June 27, 2022, per the documents. The documents also mention that Chaplain Anthony Cop officiated their wedding.
