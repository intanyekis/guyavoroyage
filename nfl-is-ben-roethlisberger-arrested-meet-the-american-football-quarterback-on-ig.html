NFL: Is Ben Roethlisberger Arrested? Meet The American Football Quarterback On IG

Fans wonder if Ben Roethlisberger was arrested as the athlete nears towards his retirement from the sport.

Ben Roethlisberger is an American professional football player who plays for Pittsburgh Steelers in the NFL.

Playing as a quarterback, Roethlisberger has been performing at the top level for more than 15 years now.

He was first selected by the Steelers back in 2004 in the NFL draft and since then the athlete has played for them throughout his career.

With around 17 years’ contribution to the Steelers football team, Ben is now edging closer to his retirement.

The athlete is playing his final game against the Kansas City Chiefs as his contract expires after the game, meaning that he would retire.

Amid this moment, fans wonder if the athlete was ever arrested.

Ben Roethlisberger Arrested 2022- Is He A Rapist?

Thus, we cannot call him a rapist since he was not proven to be one by the court.

Ben Roethlisberger Motorcycle Injury Explored

Eventually, after spending weeks getting better, Ben eventually returned to the field.

Who Is Ben Roethlisberger Wife?

Ben Roethlisberger is together with his wife, Ashley Harlan.
