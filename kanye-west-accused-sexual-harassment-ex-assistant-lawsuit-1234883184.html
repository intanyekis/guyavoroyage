Kanye West Accused Of Sexual Harassment By Ex-Assistant In Lawsuit

Kanye West seems to be happily living his life with wife Bianca Censori, but can’t seem to escape legal woes, as he’s recently been hit with a sexual harassment lawsuit filed by his former assistant, Lauren Pisciotta. The former employee is accusing the Donda founder of masturbating in her presence.

According to legal documents, Pisciotta was already making “seven figures” on OnlyFans when she was asked to work with Ye in 2021. She claims West offered her $1,000,000 to drop her adult-content job and become his “God like” executive/personal assistant. Pisciotta said that the job description consisted of being on-call for the Chicagoan 24/7.

The woman says that when she began working for Yeezy, he started to behave inappropriately, including sending her explicit text messages and pleasuring himself. Pisciotta claims that he sent her one message that read: “See my problem is I be wanting to f**k but then after I f**k I want a girl to tell me how hard they been f**ked while I’m f**king them. Then I want her to cheat on me.”

In another message, she alleges that the ex-husband of Kim Kardashian wrote: “Is my d*ck racist? I’m going to stare at pictures of white women with black a**es and beat the sh*t out of my racist d*ck.”

Additionally, the former assistant says West was “fixated” on knowing the size of her boyfriend’s penis and would unsolicitedly send her footage of him sexually engaging with models.

Pisciotta recalled two times witnessing the 46-year-old pleasuring himself – once while on the phone with her, and another time when he allegedly locked himself in a room with her during a private flight to Paris. She says the Grammy-winner then laid down on a bed and masturbated under covers until he fell asleep.

According to Pisciotta, Ye let her go in October 2022 after she allegedly turned down a $4,000,000 offer to be his Chief of Staff.

The adult-content creator is now suing West for wrongful termination and breach of contract. She claims she’s owed a $3,000,000 severance package that was promised, but never paid.
