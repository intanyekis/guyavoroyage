Ryan Clady is an American football player with an estimated net worth of $40 million.

Ryan Clady played for Eisenhower High School's football team. He was also a first-team All-Citrus Belt League selection, a first-team All-San Bernardino County selection, and a first-team All-California Interscholastic Federation Division I selection.

From 2004 to 2007, Clady played for Boise State Broncos. He was redshirted during his first year, earned first team All-Western Athletic Conference honors in his sophomore season, and was named to the Second-team All-American squad.

In 2008, Clady was picked during the first round of the NFL Draft by the Denver Broncos. He became a three-time Pro Bowl selection.

Ryan Jacob Clady was born on September 6, 1986 in Long Beach, California. He attended Boise State University in Idaho.
