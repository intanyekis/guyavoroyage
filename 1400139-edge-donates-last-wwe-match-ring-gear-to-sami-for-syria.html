Edge Donates Last WWE Match Ring Gear To Sami For Syria

Edge is having Sami Zayn auction off his gear from potentially his final WWE match for a great cause.

RAW Superstar Sami Zayn took to social media to announce that WWE Hall of Famer Edge has autographed and donated his Toronto gear from his SmackDown match with Sheamus to auction off for Sami For Syria.

“Edge is a fantastic guy. He got special ring gear made for his last event in Toronto, wore it, signed it & is now auctioning it off, with 100% of proceeds going to http://SamiForSyria.com to fund our mobile clinics in Syria. Much *red heart emoji* @EdgeRatedR. Stay tuned for a link to bid soon. By the way, I didn’t ask him to do this. It’s something he set up internally with WWE Auctions and Fanatics. I only found out after the fact from them. He went out of his way to do it,” Sami Zayn said in a series of posts earlier today.

When Sami Zayn provides the link to this auction, this article will be updated.
