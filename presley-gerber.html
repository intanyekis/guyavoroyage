Presley Gerber News - Us Weekly

Name:Presley Gerber

Hometown:Los Angeles, CA

Relationship:Single

Birthday:July 2, 1999

About

The son of supermodel Cindy Crawford and businessman Rande Gerber, Presley is a model and the older brother of fellow model Kaia Gerber. Presley won the Emerging Model Award at the 2017 Daily Front Row Awards. Over the years, he has modeled for Prabal Gurung, Sies Marjan, Moschino, Superdry and Dolce & Gabbana, and he has covered issues of T Singapore, GQ Style Mexico and L’Officiel Hommes. He also starred alongside Crawford in a 2018 Pepsi commercial. In December of that year, he was arrested for driving under the influence in Beverly Hills, and in 2019, he took a plea deal in the case, agreeing to three years of probation. In 2020, he made headlines for getting the word “misunderstood” tattooed on his face.
