Interacting with a Meditation Point will save the game, and access several other game options such as: Stances, Rest, Perks, Training, Skills or Fast Travel. Resting at a Meditation Point will restore Cal's Health, Force and Stims, as well as respawn all enemies.

How to Fast Travel in Star Wars Jedi: Survivor?

This option will be unlocked soon after you reach the planet Koboh for the first time. You can Fast Travel between Meditation Points located on the planet you are currently on. Sit at the Meditation Point, and select the Fast Travel option on the lower-right side. You will see a list of all Meditation Points you have been to, located on the same planet you are on. Just select the one you want to go to.
