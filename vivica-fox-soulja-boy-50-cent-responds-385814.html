Vivica A. Fox Apologizes To Soulja Boy, Responds To 50 Cent

As if 50 Cent didn’t have enough beef on his hands (see: his recent online spat with Rick Ross), the G-Unit general engaged in yet another war of words – this time with Vivica A. Fox. The actress appeared on Bravo’s Watch What Happens Live and discussed 50’s commentary about “gay stuff” on the FOX hit series, Empire. Things took a turn when Fox insinuated that the rap mogul himself was gay, referencing a 2010 XXL featuring 50 Cent and Soulja Boy.

“He just looked like a booty-snatcher on that one to me,” she told host Andy Cohen.

50 Cent took to Instagram to respond – as everyone knew he would – and Soulja Boy also got in on the spat. The Atlanta rapper called Fox a “granny desperate for attention” and “washed up” for implicating him in the matter. Catching wind of their clapbacks on Monday (Nov. 9), Fox issued an apology to Soulja Boy, and denied 50’s claim, “I let her lick my a**.” Noting that she intended to aim her commentary at 50, she also wrote that the G-Unit boss’ response showed that he was “in his feelings.”
