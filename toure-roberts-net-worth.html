Toure Roberts Wiki Biography

Toure Roberts was born on the 8th September 1972, in Oakland, California USA, and is a pastor, author and life coach, best known to the world for founding the One Church International in 2002, which since then has become one of the fastest growing churches in Los Angeles. He is also known for his books and owns and operates a website, AreYouWhole.com.

Have you ever wondered how rich Roberts’ endeavors have made him? As of early 2018, Roberts’ net worth is estimated to be as high as $3 million by authoritative sources.
