Dua Lipa Seems To Have A Thing For Very, Very, Rich Men, Here's Who She's Dated

English singer and songwriter, Dua Lipa, is very famous for her hit songs and her signature sound. Lipa started singing at a very young age. At 14, she regularly covered music and posted it on her YouTube channel. When she was 15, she was approached to model and had a brief career modeling. Nevertheless, she never gave up on her dream of being a singer.

At the age of 20, Warner Music Group signed her, and she released her first single, "New Love". From there on, her career took off, and she has been topping the charts ever since. She has made a net worth of $35 million and won many awards.

Lipa has been nominated for a Grammy eight times, and she won three of them. Once as a new artist, another for the best pop vocal album, and the third Grammy for dance recording. However, her music isn't the only thing that has been keeping Lipa in the headlines. She has been gaining attention for her taste in men and for having rich boyfriends.

Dua Lipa Had An On And Off Relationship With Isaac Carew

Carew is an English model and a chef as well. He has been modeling since 2008 and has a thriving career. Working with some of the biggest modeling agencies, such as ' Next New York', 'Scoop Models', 'Next Milan,' and much more, he gathered a net worth of $4 million.

His relationship with Lipa was a rollercoaster. The couple met in 2013 and officially became together in 2015. Two years later, they separated, only to be together on and off. That was until they officially broke up in 2019. The reason for the breakup was reportedly their overlapping busy schedules.

Dua Lipa Dated Paul Klein For A Couple Of Months

Amidst Lipa's unsteady relationship with Carew, she dated Klein, the lead singer and guitarist of LANY, an indie-pop band. The singer makes his income through his band, ad campaigns, photoshoots, modeling, and brand endorsements. He has a total net worth of $1.5 million.

The couple dated for a short period of time, from the summer of 2017 to January 2018. It was Lipa who ended the relationship to get back with Carew. The breakup was hard on Klein. In an interview with Harpers Bazaar, the singer expressed how he fell in love with Lipa fast, and how painful the breakup was for him.

Dua Lipa Dated Anwar Hadid For More Than Two Years

Known for being an American-Palestinian model, Hadid is mostly famous for being a Hadid. He's the brother of the supermodels, Gigi and Bella Hadid. He made a name for himself when he got signed with the modeling agency, IMG Models. Dabbling in music as well, he gathered a net worth of $4 million. However, he became under the spotlight when he started dating Lipa in 2019.

The iconic couple has made headlines so many times over a period of two years. They were always pictured together walking hand in hand and being affectionate. They dressed cute together, attended events, pictured laughing, and Lipa was even close to his family and especially his mother, Yolanda Hadid.

Dua Lipa And Trevor Noah Were Spotted Kissing

The South African comedian, producer, writer, actor, television host, and political commentator made a huge name for himself. Having been in the USA for around 11 years, Noah has many awards to his name, including a Primetime Emmy Award. He is by far Lipa's richest rumored boyfriend, as he has a net worth of $100 million.

While Lipa said that she has been happy to be single for a while, she was spotted kissing Noah outside a restaurant in New York. The PDA-packed pictures sparked romance rumors between the 26-year-old singer and the 38-year-old TV host. The couple dressed casually and looked very comfortable around each other, before they shared a hug and a kiss.
